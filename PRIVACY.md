## Introduction

Portuguese Verbs ("we", "our", or "us") is an app for learning Portuguese verb conjugations. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Data Collected Automatically

Our app may collect anonymous usage statistics and crash reports to improve the application:

- **Firebase Analytics** (if enabled): We use Firebase Analytics to understand how users interact with our app, including:
  - Feature usage patterns (lessons, exercises, vocabulary)
  - Session duration
  - User engagement metrics

- **Firebase Crashlytics** (if enabled): We collect crash reports to identify and fix bugs:
  - Crash logs
  - Device information (model, OS version)
  - Stack traces

### Data Stored Locally

All learning progress and user data is stored locally on your device:

- Lesson progress (tenses, completed lessons)
- Exercise and study statistics
- Vocabulary and verb learning lists (words/verbs you mark for study)
- App settings (language, theme, exercise count, etc.)

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

## How We Use Your Information

Collected data is used solely for:

- **Improving app stability** – Identifying and fixing bugs
- **Understanding user behavior** – Enhancing the learning experience
- **Feature development** – Making informed decisions about app improvements (e.g. lessons, tenses, exercises)

## Data Storage and Security

- Analytics data (if any) is stored on Google Firebase servers and handled according to [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- Local app data is stored on your device; we do not have access to it
- We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties

## User Rights

You have the right to:

- Opt out of analytics data collection through your device settings
- Request information about data we collect

### How to Disable Analytics

You can disable analytics in your device settings:

- **Android**: Settings → Apps → Portuguese Verbs → Permissions → Disable Analytics (if available)

## Children's Privacy

Our app is designed for users of all ages who wish to learn Portuguese. We comply with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act):

- We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13
- If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately
